## 5. Timeline

- **Phase 1:** Approximately 2 weeks from receipt of Payment 1.
- **Phase 2:** Approximately 2 weeks from receipt of Payment 2.
- **Total estimated calendar time:** Approximately 4–5 weeks from kickoff.
- **Delays caused by Client:** If Client delays providing assets, access, or feedback for more than 3 business days, the timeline extends by the delay duration plus 2 business days.

## 6. Acceptance Criteria

- **Phase 1 acceptance:** Client shall review the delivered Phase 1 features within 5 business days of delivery. If no blocking issues are reported in writing within that window, Phase 1 is considered accepted.
- **Phase 2 / Final acceptance:** Client shall review the complete platform within 5 business days of final delivery. If no blocking issues are reported in writing within that window, the project is considered complete.
- **Blocking issue:** A defect that prevents core functionality from working as specified in the Scope Document. Minor visual inconsistencies or non-core feature gaps do not constitute blocking issues.
